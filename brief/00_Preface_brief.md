## §0.1 What the framework is

The GSF is a formal structural algebraic model for coherent entities. Any coherent entity is characterized by:

- **{S, A, I, R}** — four irreducible intrinsic attributes: Singularity (scalar self-boundary), directed operative vector, Impulse (intrinsic orientation), and relational coupling.
- **Force** = S·**A** (polar vector) and **Field** = **I** ∧ **R** (bivector) — inherent, non-eliminable operations.
- **Purpose** P — an inherent attractor as a capacity, contextually instantiated as ξ*(ρ).
- **ρ** — a contextual constraint that determines D_X(ρ).
- **Γ = Γ_s + Γ_a** — a structural and orientational coupling matrix.

The framework proposes that biological organization and psychological dynamics *may admit* a common structural representation as instantiations of the same algebraic language.

**Formal Core vs. Interpretative Layer:**

| Stratum | Content |
|---|---|
| Formal Core | {S, A, I, R}, Force, Field, Γ, ρ, P, ξ, Γ_E(Γ), Ω |
| Interpretative Layer | Biological mappings (autopoiesis); psychological readings (agency, love, wisdom) |

## §0.2 Scope

**It Models:** minimal structural organization of coherent entities; purpose dynamics as an attractor; correspondences at biological and psychological levels; experiential spectra (peace–suffering, love–survival, worth–shame) as attractor landscapes.

**It does not claim to explain:**
1. The ontological nature of consciousness — agnostic between panpsychism, emergentism, and functionalism.
2. The hard problem of subjective experience.
3. The phenomenological quality of conscious states.

**Γ_E(Γ) — modulation operator.** Γ describes what the system *is*; Γ_E(Γ) describes how the system processes its own Γ. Effective dynamics are governed by Γ_eff = Γ_E(Γ). When there is no active epistemological level, Γ_E = Id and Γ_eff = Γ. With self-awareness, Γ_E redirects dynamics without amplifying structural capacity: |**A**_eff| ≤ |**A**|. Internal constitution of Γ_E: formally open.

## §0.3 Structural Minimality Criterion

Three conditions on 𝒜 = {S, **A**, **I**, **R**} in G(3):

**C1 — Algebraic and Semantic Independence:** No attribute is derivable from the other three (a) as a grade-preserving operation in G(3), nor (b) functionally — each contributes a distinct structural capacity. Force (polar vector, grade 1) and Field (bivector, grade 2) are a concrete realization of this independence.

**C2 — Completeness** *(conjecture)*: Every structural descriptor at any level is expressible as a function of 𝒜 under the map σ_L. A fifth generator is not required.

**C3 — Uniqueness of mapping**: For any coherent entity, the assignment Φ_L : ℰ_L → {S, A, I, R} is forced by C1 and C2. If two inequivalent assignments produce structurally indistinguishable predictions, the framework declares its own application indeterminate at that level.

*Coherent entity*: a system where (i) {S, A, I, R} are simultaneously definable and mutually non-degenerate, and (ii) Force and Field are simultaneously active. Autopoietic systems are the paradigmatic case at the biological level.

**Falsification criteria:**

| Violated condition | Implication |
|---|---|
| C1 | Redundant algebraic base; G(3) requires replacement |
| C2 | Incomplete framework at that level; may require G(4) |
| C3 | Application beyond valid domain; insufficient correspondence rules |
